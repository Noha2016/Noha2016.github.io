Oven baked chicken

  • Time: 40 min
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
olive oil 2 tablespoons
light brown sugar 2 teaspoons
dried oregano 1 teaspoons
mild sweet paprika 1 teaspoons
each garlic powder and onion powder 1 teaspoons
dried thyme 1/2 teaspoons
salt 1 teaspoons
Cracked black pepper, to taste 1/4 teaspoons
chicken thighs, bone in or out 6 teaspoons

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 350째F (180째).
  2. In a shallow bowl, mix together the olive oil, sugar, oregano, paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, thyme, salt and pepper. Add the chicken and coat each piece evenly with the seasoning. Set aside, allowing the flavours to absorb into the meat. If time allows, marinade overnight for a deeper flavour.
  3. Spray a large baking dish (10-inch x 15-inch OR 25 x 35 cm) with nonstick cooking oil spray. Add the onion, garlic, oil, salt, pepper, HOT boiled broth, hot water and butter into the dish.Mix well to melt the butter and combine.
  4. Arrange thighs in the dish , cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Uncover, spray chicken with oil and bake until the chicken is cooked . Change oven setting to broil to brown the chicken (only needs 3-5 minutes).
  5. Transfer chicken onto a plate garnish with remaining chives and chopped parsley.
    SERVE and ENJOGY!

Vegetable soup

  • Time: 55 min
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
olive oil 2 tablespoons
chopped yellow onion 1 1/2 cups
peeled and chopped carrots (about 5) 2 cups
chopped celery (about 3) 1 1/4 cups
garlic , minced 4 cloves
chicken broth* or vegetable broth 4 (14.5 oz) cans
diced tomatoes 2 (14.5 oz) cans
peeled and 1/2-inch thick diced potatoes 3 cups
chopped fresh parsley 1/3 cup
bay leaves 2
dried thyme 1/2 tsp
chopped frozen or fresh green beans 1 1/2 cups
frozen or fresh corn 1 1/4 cups
frozen or fresh peas 1 cup

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
  2. Add onions, carrots, and celery and saute 4 minutes then add garlic and saute 30 seconds longer.
  3. Add in broth tomatoes, potatoes, parsley, bay leaves, thyme and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Bring to a boil, then add green beans. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover and simmer until potatoes are almst fully tender, about 20 - 30 minutes.
  5. Add corn and peas and cook 5 minutes longer.
    6. Serve warm.

garlic roasted potatoes

  • Time: 1:40 min
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
potatoes, cut into 1-inch cubes 2 pounds (1 kg)
good quality olive oil or canola oil 2 tablespoons
sea salt 1 teaspoon
freshly ground black pepper 2 pounds (1 kg)
minced garlic, (or 8 cloves garlic, minced) 2 tablespoons
fresh chopped parsley 2 tablespoons

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat the oven to 400째F (200째C).Lightly spray a baking sheet or tray with cooking oil spray.
  2. Arrange potatoes on sheet in a single layer. Drizzle with the oil and season with salt, pepper and garlic. Toss so potatoes are evenly seasoned.
  3. Roast in the oven for 45-55 minutes while flipping occasionally, until crisp and golden.
  4. Remove the potatoes from the oven and season with a little extra salt and pepper to taste. Sprinkle over with parsley and serve immediately!
